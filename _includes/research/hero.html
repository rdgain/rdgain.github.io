Research

I design Artificial Intelligence systems that can play, analyse, generate and test games. My work combines evolutionary computation, reinforcement learning, planning, simulation and machine learning to build robust decision-making systems across both digital and tabletop games.

Beginning with my PhD research on Rolling Horizon Evolutionary Algorithms for General Video Game Playing, my research has expanded into AI-driven simulation platforms, automated playtesting, digital tabletop games, explainable AI and large language models. Throughout, my focus has remained on creating practical AI systems that support designers, researchers and players.
